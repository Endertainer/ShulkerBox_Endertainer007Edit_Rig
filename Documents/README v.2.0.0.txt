> What is this Rig?
This rig is a custom version of DarthLilo's Shulker Box R1.0.1 Rig.

> What's new in this version?
-Hybrid IK+FK Pivot
-Improved Face Panel
-PROP Panel Toggle
-Extrudable Mustache
-Mustache Tracker
-Arm IK/FK Snapping & Notifier
-Head/Hat Layer Lattice Deform & Switch
-Improved Emotion & Eyelid Control
-Blink Control Eyelid Rock & Slide
-Top/Both Eyelid Blink Switch
-Mouth Switch
-Eye Tracker & Switch
-Eyebrow Tracker
-Emotion Tracker
-Arm Stretch Switch
-Glowing Eye Shader
